Why This Matters

The South builds big. It just needs a better way to connect.

In the north, communities form naturally through recurring events, founder circles, and networking platforms. In Southern Taiwan, that connective layer is still thin, even though the talent, research, and industrial depth are already here.

STTC exists to help close that gap: making it easier for students, builders, researchers, and operators in the South to find one another, exchange ideas, and build momentum together.

Purpose

One platform for Southern Taiwan’s mission and vision to move together.

Mission

Build the connective layer.

STTC exists to strengthen connection across Southern Taiwan’s tech landscape by making it easier for founders, students, researchers, operators, and allies to find one another, share ideas, and build together.

Vision

Make the South visible.

We create the public-facing layer for tech in the South, making innovation more visible, connected, and cross-disciplinary across semiconductor, AI, sustainability, and the wider regional ecosystem.

Rooted in the South

Built around the places where Southern Taiwan innovates.

The science parks and industrial corridors of the South are already powering global technology. What is missing is a stronger public-facing community around them.

STTC helps turn that physical ecosystem into a social one, connecting the people building inside it.

The Ecosystem

What STTC builds across Southern Taiwan.

STTC is a connector across the South’s most important technology verticals, helping communities, talent, and ideas move more fluidly between research, startups, industry, and public-facing dialogue.

Semiconductor

We surface the people, labs, suppliers, and operators shaping the semiconductor ecosystem, creating more visibility and stronger cross-sector relationships around the region’s industrial backbone.

AI

We create space for AI builders, researchers, and founders to meet, share work, and collaborate across disciplines so innovation in the South feels connected rather than scattered.

Sustainability

We highlight solutions that link technological growth with long-term resilience, helping conversations around climate, infrastructure, and responsible innovation become part of the ecosystem itself.
